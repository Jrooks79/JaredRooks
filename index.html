Based in St. Louis, Missouri, Jared Rooks is a session musician and recording artist available for recording sessions, live performance, touring, and fill-in opportunities.

Experience

From intimate club stages to arena-sized productions, Jared Rooks has built a reputation as a dependable and versatile session musician.
